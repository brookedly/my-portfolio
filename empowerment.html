What I Stand For

DIVERSITY IN TECH

Diversity in tech is a hot topic throughout the world. Therefore, I am a strong advocate for diversity in tech - whether to you that means culturally or gender specific. I am currently a member in WICS (Women In Computer Science) club on campus as well as an ICS/BRAID Scholarship recipient that allowed me to attend the 2017 Grace Hopper Conference. Recently, I participated in an outreach workshop called igniteCS which focused on educating the younger generation the importance of the growing field of technology. As said by Apple and other American tech companies "diversity makes us stronger."

TECH INCLUSION

Beyond diversity in tech, I am also passionate about tech inclusion, more specifically on how tech inclusion primarily focuses on thriving education to bring awareness to STEM related fields, embracing all different ethnic, genders, and backgrounds at a workplace, and having an accepting work policy.

CATTLE AGRICULTURE'S IMPACT

Before watching this film, I wasn't aware of how beef was being produced and how much of an impact consumption factors into one's health and the world. Beinging a meat eater originally, I wasn't excited about watching the film. But once I started watching more and more vegan YouTubers, I became more open toward the idea of it. Eventually, I watched it. Although I am not an extremeist when it comes to veganism - I do highly encourage people to educate themselves of where and how their source of food is coming from and how it may effect multiple area of a person's live.
